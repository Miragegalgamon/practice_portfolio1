About me

My name is Miraj Mahabub Samee, and I am an aspiring digital artist, graphics designer & web developer. My journey began with a fascination for 2D art in my childhood, which later grew into a passion for various creative disciplines. At SMUCT, I broadened my interests to include design and 3D modeling, enriching my artistic skills.

Recently, my fascination has turned toward web development, where I see the opportunity to blend my artistic vision with technical prowess. The process of creating visually captivating and user-friendly websites intrigues me, and I am eager to dive into this field. I look forward to crafting engaging digital experiences that combine aesthetics with practical functionality, contributing to the evolving landscape of web design.
